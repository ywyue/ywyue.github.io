About Me

I am a research scientist intern at Meta Reality Labs and an incoming ELLIS PhD student at ETH Zurich and the University of Oxford, supervised by Prof. Konrad Schindler and Prof. Christian Rupprecht. I obtained my Master's degree from ETH Zurich, mainly under the supervision of Dr. Francis Engelmann, Dr. Theodora Kontogianni, Prof. Siyu Tang and Prof. Konrad Schindler. Prior to that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Wuhan University and interned at the University of Alberta and DeepMap.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, computer graphics and machine learning, with a goal of learning to build and understand the 3D world. I am particularly enthusiastic about digitizing of real-world environments and semantic scene interpretation.
